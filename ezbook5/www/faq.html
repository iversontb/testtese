HOW TO REGISTER

How can I register to use the ezBook by ABC.?

ezBook by ABC. is available to any agent/agency worldwide via registration. Go to http://www.ezbook.travel.

A one-time online registration is required by the agency owner, as well as individual agents. On the login page, the agency owner or designated manager/administrator will click the .Agency Registration. link. Complete the required fields, click the Terms and Conditions box, then click the .Register. box to complete your registration. Please note the following password guidelines:

Â·         Passwords must be at least 8 characters long

Â·         Passwords cannot exceed 20 characters in length

Â·         Passwords must contain at least one of each of the following: symbol (! @ $ _ - + &); upper-case letter; lower-case letter; number.

Once Agency Registration is complete, individual agents can then register. Click the link .Agent Registration.. Once all required fields (indicated by an asterisk) are completed, click the .Register. box and you are ready to book.

Can an individual agent register with ezBook by ABC. if the agency has not registered?

Yes. Agents can register and begin booking with ezBook by ABC., but please note that commissions cannot be paid to the agency/agent until the agency has registered.

INVENTORY TYPE - GDS RATES

What are GDS rates?

Used today by many traditional travel management companies (TMCs), these are rates that hotels provide via the Global Distribution Systems (GDSs). This includes Amadeus, Galileo/Apollo, Sabre and Worldspan.

What rates are available via the GDS inventory category?

Indicated by inventory type , the GDS rates include ABC Premier Hotel Program rates (ABC and AOM rate codes), Premier Privileges by ABC (PSQ rate code) as well as AAA rates, government rates, senior rates, special packages, promotional and seasonal rates.

 

Are GDS reservations prepaid?

Not all GDS reservations are pre-paid. Keep in mind that if you are booking an advance purchase rate, payment is required at time of booking.

 

What is the commission on GDS reservations?

8% commission is paid on all GDS bookings.

 

When will I receive my commission for GDS bookings?

 

Commissions are paid following the completion of the guest.s stay and once ABC Global Services has been paid by the hotel/chain. ABC pays commissions on the 15th and 30th of every month with monies received by the 12th or 27th as the cutoff for processing. Should the 15th or 30th fall on a weekend, payment will be made the next business day. ABC always supports our customers by tracking all commissions on their behalf. Note: if the reservation is canceled or there is a no-show, the commission is forfeited.

 

Can I establish my own commission for GDS reservations?

No. Hotels set the commissions on all GDS bookings. This is not flexible.

 

However, a competitive benefit of ezBook is that you can add a service fee! No other provider offers this option that supports your revenue-generating goals.

NOTE: With ABC.s Pre-Paid Net rates you can set your own commission.

How do I track my commissions?

As with all bookings made using ezBook by ABC., you can track and view the status of all bookings and commissions via our real-time reports in the reporting module. Within the header across the top of the page click .Administration. and the .Transaction Reports. tab.

 

What is the traveler's payment responsibility for GDS reservations?

As with all GDS bookings, a credit card number is required to guarantee the hotel. The traveler is responsible for payment upon checkout.

After logging in to ezBook, you will be on the .Book Hotels. page. You can begin booking your hotels from here. You have the option to search for a hotel by city name, airport name, and landmark . which is a featured option not offered on most sites.

 

How many rooms can be booked in one reservation?

This is based on the type of inventory. In short:

Â·         GDS: one room per availability request

Â·         Web Discount: eight rooms maximum

Â·         Pre-Paid Net and Pre-Paid ez Net: eight rooms maximum

What if I have a special client request?

Requests for smoking/non-smoking rooms, specific bed types, room types, etc. should be made at the time of booking within the Advance Search option.

 

GDS bookings: special requests are guaranteed at the time of booking and based on availability.

 

Web bookings: special requests are handled only at the time of check-in. Note that overall, special requests are usually based on availability at the time of check-in and provided at the discretion of the property.

 

Can I book a group reservation on ezBook?

No, however, many vendors allow you to book up to eight rooms at a time.

MODIFY OR CANCEL A HOTEL RESERVATION

Can I modify an existing hotel reservation?

 

Once a reservation has been made, agents will need to cancel the original reservation and make a new reservation. See .how do I cancel a reservation once booked?..

How do I cancel a reservation once booked?

 

To cancel a reservation, click .Administration. within the header at the top of the page. Click on the .Transaction Reports. tab, then click the confirmation number and click .cancel.. As with your original reservation, you will receive an email confirming the cancellation. If you made a reservation using a Pre-Paid rate, the booking can be cancelled but the rate is non-refundable.

Note: Each hotel and/or chain has their own cancellation and change policy. ABC Global Services does not add any additional fees to a cancellation or change to a hotel. Penalties for cancellations are typically the rate for one night if cancelling 24-48 hours prior to arrival but these rates may vary based on market and time of year. For GDS advance purchase rates, many are non-refundable and many Web discounts are completely non-refundable.
