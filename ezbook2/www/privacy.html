Privacy Policy

INTRODUCTION

This Site understands the importance of your right to privacy and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

This Site collects personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To identify, contact and provide purchased service to customers
  • To establish and maintain responsible commercial relations with customers and to provide ongoing service and offers
  • To address special customer needs
  • To manage and develop this Site's business and operations
  • To meet legal and regulatory requirements.

This Site's policy will apply to and protect all personal information collected used or disclosed by this Site when an individual:

  • Makes an inquiry or travel reservation through our website
  • Registers or provides information by email or through the internet
  • Inquires about his/her services
  • Makes additional requests for services
  • Applies payment to an existing travel reservation
  • Issues a complaint regarding his/her services
  • Signs a contract or other form of authorization

THE PRIVACY PRINCIPLES THIS SITE FOLLOWS

There are ten principles, which form the basis of this Site's policy. These principles are interrelated and this Site shall adhere to them as a whole. As permitted by law, the interpretation of this Site's policy may be tailored to reflect personal information issues specific to this Site.

Principle 1 - Accountability
This Site is responsible for personal information under its control.

Principle 2 - Identifying Purposes for Collection of Personal Information
This Site shall identify the purposes for which personal information is collected at or before the time the information is collected.

Principle 3 - Obtaining Consent for Collection, Use or Disclosure of Personal Information
The knowledge and consent of a customer is required for the collection, use, or disclosure of personal information, except where inappropriate.

Principle 4 - Limiting Collection
This Site will limit the collection of personal information to that which is necessary for the identified purposes and will collect this information by fair and lawful means. Personal information which may fall under the policy may include but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Telephone number
  • Email address
  • Gender
  • Nationality
  • Medical/Dietary Requirements
  • Credit card information
  • Emergency Contact
  • Personal Preferences for Hotel/Airline
  • Hotel Room #'s
  • Pre-assigned aircraft seats
  • Destination incident report information
  • Opinions as consensually given on questionnaires
  • Banking information
  • Language preference
  • Passport information
  • Frequent Flyer Number

Principle 5 - Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention of Personal Information
This Site will not use or disclose personal information for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with the consent of the individual or as required by law. This Site will retain personal information only as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes, or as required by law. This Site may disclose a customer's personal information to: * A third party for provision of any of This Site's products or services for which the information was expressly collected (including hoteliers, airlines, ground handlers) * A third party as required by law or emergency * A third party retained by This Site to perform functions on its behalf.

Principle 6 - Accuracy of Personal Information
Personal information shall be as accurate, complete, and up-to-date as is necessary for the purposes for which it is to be used.

Principle 7 - Security Safeguards
This Site shall protect personal information by security safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information. These safeguards may include:

  • Physical measures (locked filing cabinets, on-site security)
  • Technological tools (database passwords)
  • Organizational controls (staff training, confidentiality agreements)

Principle 8 - Openness Concerning Policies and Practices
This Site will make readily available to customers and employees specific information about its policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.

Principle 9 - Customer Access to Personal Information
This Site shall inform a customer of the existence, use, and disclosure of his or her personal information upon request and shall give the individual access to that information. A customer shall be able to challenge the accuracy and completeness of the information and have it amended as appropriate.

Principle 10 - Challenging Compliance
An individual shall be able to address a challenge concerning compliance with the above principles to info@ezbook.travel or +1.312.462.1976
